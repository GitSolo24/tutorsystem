About Inter-School Online Tutoring

Inter-school Tutoring System is a platform that connects students with tutors for online tutoring sessions. This will help to find tutors from nearby locations. Tutors can also get student just by logging into the website and setting up the profile. In the system there are three entity namely, Admin, Students and Tutors. Admin can login, manage tutor by adding new teachers and updating their profiles. Admin can also check for the registered students. Admin will register tutors and credentials will be shared to tutors by Email. Students can register and login, tutors can be viewed by students. Students can filter and select the tutor and after selecting students will raise the request of demo lecture. After attending lecture, they can book the tutor online and rate the tutor. Tutor can login by using credentials which will be provided by mail. They can check for the request for demo lecture and accept the request. They can also check the booking done. They need to set their profile. This system can help the tutors to get students to find the best tutors.
